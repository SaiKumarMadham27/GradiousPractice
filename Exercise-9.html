BIO

Allu Arjun was born on 8 April 1982 in a Telugu family in Madras (present-day Chennai) to film producer Allu Aravind and Nirmala.
His paternal grandfather was the noted film comedian Allu Ramalingaiah who appeared in over 1000 films.
Their native place is Palakollu of West Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh.Allu Arjun grew up in Chennai before their family moved to Hyderabad in his 20s.
He is the second of three children. His elder brother Venkatesh is a businessman while his younger brother Sirish is an actor.
His paternal aunt is married to Chiranjeevi. His cousins, namely, Ram Charan, Varun Tej, Sai Dharam Tej, and Niharika Konidela are also actors.
